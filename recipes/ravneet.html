Ingredients

  • 250g unsalted butter, softened
  • 250g golden caster sugar
  • 4 eggs

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 200C (180C fan)/350F/gas 4 and grease and line the bottom and sides of a 20cm cake tin with greaseproof paper.
  2. Make the toffee sauce by mixing all of the ingredients in a saucepan and bringing to a gentle simmer. Stir well, take off the heat, then leave to cool.
  3. Put the butter and sugar in a bowl and beat until pale and fluffy. Beat in the eggs one at a time, followed by the lemon zest and vanilla extract. Fold in the diced apples, followed by the flour and salt, and mix well.
